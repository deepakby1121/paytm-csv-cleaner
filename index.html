Paytm CSV Cleaner

Choose your Paytm CSV. This page keeps only the final required columns, groups by POS_ID in this order: PUMP-01, PUMP-02, then others/blank; sorts by Transaction_Date inside each group; converts the time to 12-hour AM/PM format; and downloads a cleaned CSV.

Your file stays on this device. Nothing is uploaded anywhere.

Select a Paytm CSV to start.
Output columns

Transaction_Date, Order_ID, Transaction_ID, Status, Amount, Settled_Amount, Payment_Mode, Bank_Transaction_ID, Merchant_Name, POS_ID, Customer_VPA
